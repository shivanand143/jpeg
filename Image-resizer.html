Frequently Asked Questions

What is a JPEG compressor and resizer?

A JPEG compressor and resizer is a tool used to reduce the file size and dimensions of JPEG images, making them more suitable for sharing and displaying on the web.

How does your compressor and resizer work?

Our compressor and resizer uses advanced algorithms to analyze and reduce the size of JPEG images without sacrificing quality. We also allow users to specify custom dimensions for their images.

Is my data safe with your compressor and resizer?

Yes, we take data privacy and security very seriously. All images uploaded to our website are encrypted and stored securely on our servers. We also do not share any user data with third parties.

What is the maximum file size I can upload?

Our website allows users to upload JPEG images up to 5MB in size. If you have a larger file, we recommend using a desktop application or online tool designed for bulk image compression.

Can I compress and resize multiple images at once?

Currently, our website only allows users to compress and resize one image at a time. However, we are working on adding a batch processing feature in the near future.
